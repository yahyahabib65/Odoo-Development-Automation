Purpose: Currently, auth failures produce generic "GitHub token required" messages. The wizard diagnoses the specific failure (gh not installed vs not authenticated vs token invalid) and provides targeted remediation steps, making the first-run experience smooth.

Output: New wizard module, updated CLI commands with --no-wizard flag, comprehensive unit tests.
</objective>
