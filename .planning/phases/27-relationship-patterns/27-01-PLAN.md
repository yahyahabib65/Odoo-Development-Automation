<objective>
Implement relationship pattern support: through-models for M2M with extra fields, self-referential M2M with explicit relation/column params, and hierarchical parent_id/parent_path models.

Purpose: Enables the generator to produce three advanced Odoo relationship patterns that users currently cannot express in specs, preventing 4-5 subtle bugs per pattern (cascade deletion, table name collisions, parent_path indexing, missing ACLs, missing init imports).
Output: Updated renderer.py with _process_relationships() preprocessor, updated model.py.j2 templates for both 17.0 and 18.0, comprehensive unit and integration tests.
</objective>
